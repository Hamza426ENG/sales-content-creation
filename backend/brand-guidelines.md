# Edge Brand Guidelines

## Company
- **Name:** Edge
- **Tagline:** Hire World-Class Talent, Fully Managed
- **Website:** onedge.co
- **Headquarters:** 800 W El Camino Real, Suite 180, Mountain View, CA 94040

## Brand Voice & Tone
- Professional, confident, and results-oriented
- Use clear, direct language — avoid jargon
- Emphasize value: cost savings (60-70% lower cost), quality (HIPAA-trained), and reliability (fully managed)
- Focus on healthcare, dental, and insurance verticals
- Always position Edge as a trusted partner, not just a vendor

## Key Messaging Points
- World-class, HIPAA-trained talent
- Campus-based professionals (not freelancers)
- Fully managed — Edge handles hiring, training, and oversight
- 60-70% cost reduction vs. US-based staffing
- Serving healthcare, dental, and insurance industries

## Visual Style
- **Primary Color:** Black (#000000)
- **Secondary Color:** Navy (#1E3A5F)
- **Accent Color:** Emerald Green (#10B981)
- **Font:** Open Sans (headings and body)
- Clean, minimal design with generous whitespace
- Use accent green for emphasis, highlights, and CTAs
