# Edge Brand Guidelines

## Company
- **Name:** Edge (legal: Edge Services and Solutions, LLC)
- **Tagline:** Match Your Ambition
- **Value Prop:** Hire World-Class Talent, Fully Managed
- **Website:** onedge.co
- **Headquarters:** 800 El Camino Real, Suite 180, Mountain View, CA 94040

## Brand Voice & Tone
- Professional, confident, and results-oriented
- Use clear, direct language — avoid jargon
- Lead with outcomes and data (e.g., "98% collection rate", "90% backlog reduction")
- Emphasize value: cost savings (40-70% lower cost), quality (college-educated, healthcare-trained), and reliability (fully managed, campus-based)
- Include real testimonials and case study references where appropriate
- Focus on healthcare, dental, and insurance verticals

## Key Messaging Points
- College-educated, healthcare-trained professionals
- Campus-based teams (not freelancers) — dedicated, not shared
- Fully managed — Edge handles hiring, training, oversight, and retention
- 40-70% cost reduction vs. U.S.-based staffing without compromising quality
- 4-week role-based training and certification before joining client teams
- HIPAA certified, BAA compliant, secure VPN, biometric campus access
- EHR ready: Epic, Cerner, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, NextGen, Kareo, AdvancedMD, DrChrono
- Revenue Cycle support: claims, coding, A/R, denials, insurance verification, prior auth

## Key Stats to Reference
- 98% collection rate
- 95% first pass rate
- 70% faster hiring compared to industry average
- 90% reduction in unpaid claims backlog
- 40% reduction in operational costs

## Document Structure Guidelines
- Start with a compelling headline and subtitle
- Use dotted line dividers between sections
- Group related items in card-style sections
- Include a stats/metrics section with bold numbers
- Include client testimonials in callout boxes
- End with a clear call-to-action

## Visual Style
- **Primary Color:** Purple (#5B21B6) — used for all headings and key accents
- **Dark sections:** Dark purple (#3B0764, #1E0A3C) — used for stats bars and closing sections
- **Background sections:** Lavender (#F3E8FF) — cover pages and highlight areas
- **Accent:** Emerald Green (#10B981) — underlines, bullet markers, card borders
- **Card backgrounds:** Light green (#ECFDF5) — for grouped content and callout boxes
- **Stats/Numbers:** Gold/Amber (#D97706) — for key metrics and statistics
- **Font:** Open Sans (headings bold, body regular)
- Clean, modern design with rounded corners on cards
- Dotted line dividers between major sections
